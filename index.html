Family Owned!

Squealer's Hickory Smoked Bar-B-Que has been Meridian's favorite BBQ since we opened our doors back in 1998. We love our hometown and we try our best to make our customers happy and keep them coming back again and again!

We hope you will stop in and try some of our most popular items, like our juicy pulled pork, tender smoked ribs, or our homemade bannana pudding. One visit and we are certain you will be back for more.


Terry and Teresa Cranmore

In 1998, Terry and Teresa Cranmore started catering out of a custom built food truck, with a dream of one day owning their own brick and mortor resturaunt. Soon after, they leased the first home of Squealer's Hickory Smoked Bar-B-Que on North Hills Street, just accross from Sonic. Word soon spread and they were quickly bursting from the seems of that little shop and opened a second location in the College Park shopping center.

With these two locations thriving, Terry and Teresa knew their dream had come true and they worked hard to make it even better. Building a location from the ground up was no little feat, but the hard work paid off. Now Squealer's has become Meridian's place to be. Featuring amazing bar-b-que, live music, a full bar and packed with great friends.


Meridian's Best Bar-B-Que

For years Squealer's has been voted Meridian's best Bar-B-Que, and we believe it's not just about the awesome food. Squealer's dedication to the community has been shown time and time again through fundraising activities, charity events and helping those in need. This shows the true heart of a business and how much they appreciate the role the community has played in their success!
